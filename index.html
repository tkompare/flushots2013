Learn More

Welcome to the flu shot finder. If your device has GPS, you may click on the Find Me button to see locations near you. You may enter your address and click on Go to do the same. Click on All, Next 7 Days, or On A Day of the week to highlight locations available on a certain day. Blue map locations offer free flu shots for anyone six months of age or older.
